LAPD — Los Angeles Police Department

Protect and serve Los Angeles. Enforce laws, respond to emergencies, and maintain community safety.

  • Investigate incidents and enforce laws
  • Engage with the community through roleplay

CHP — California Highway Patrol

Keep highways safe, enforce traffic laws, assist with vehicle accidents, and support other departments.

  • Conduct traffic stops and enforce road laws

LASD — Los Angeles County Sheriff’s Department

Serve and protect Los Angeles County. Patrol, manage custody, and support law enforcement operations.

LADOT — Los Angeles Department of Transportation

Manage traffic flow, respond to accidents, and ensure road safety across Los Angeles. Supports law enforcement during traffic incidents.

Respond to fires, medical calls, and rescues. A crucial part of emergency services RP in Los Angeles.
